Spaghetti Carbonara
Ingredients:
- 200g spaghetti
- 100g pancetta
- 2 large eggs
- 50g grated Parmesan cheese
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 1 tbsp olive oil

Instructions:
1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions. Reserve some pasta water.
2. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Add pancetta and cook until crispy.
3. Add garlic to the pan and saut√© for 1-2 minutes.
4. In a bowl, whisk together eggs and Parmesan cheese.
5. Combine cooked spaghetti with pancetta and garlic. Remove from heat.
6. Quickly mix the egg and cheese mixture with the pasta, adding a little reserved pasta water to create a creamy sauce.
7. Season with salt and pepper, and serve immediately.
